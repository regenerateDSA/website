# Preamble
Regenerate is an eco-socialist, intersectional, and partyist caucus organizing both within and
adjacent to TCDSA. Regenerate is focused on diversifying DSA and building a mass,
movement-oriented socialist party through issue-based, agitational campaigns. We want to
establish TCDSA as a political home for our base – workers, transit riders, renters, students and
youth, people of color, and LGBTQ people – and wrestle them away from the so-called
Democratic-Farmer-Labor Party.

# For fighting climate change - by any means necessary!
Regenerate puts climate change and environmental degradation at the center of our analysis
and action. Socialism cannot be built on an unlivable planet. Combatting climate change is our
top priority. In the short term, Regenerate strongly supports efforts that will slow the effects of climate change such as expanding mass transit, congestion pricing, carbon taxation, and other
reforms; but the problem with reforms is that they are hard-won, and easily overturned. Our
ultimate goal is building a revolutionary, eco-socialist party empowered by the masses to
restructure our entire economy around the principles of degrowth to put people and planet
first.

# For a revolutionary ecosocialist party!
A socialist party is our goal. The Democratic Party manipulates the working class - it channels
their frustrations, aspirations, and energy into supporting politicians who serve the liberal wing
of the ruling class. We believe that, in order to build this party, it is necessary to instigate a
break from the Democratic-Farmer-Labor Party. For the time being, it is necessary to work
within the DFL to (1) prevent the DFL from endorsing against TCDSA-endorsed candidates for
office, (2) change local and state electoral laws to reduce the influence of money in politics and
create a more democratic and proportional electoral system, (3) enact reforms that slow the
effects of climate change, and (4) expose the millions of workers who still believe in the
Democratic Party to our ecosocialist politics.

# For a multiracial, working class TCDSA!
The multiracial working class is our home. DSA too often fails to prioritize the recruitment and
inclusion of the most oppressed sections of the multiracial working class. Our analysis has
centered the people in power, rather than those who need power. Regenerate seeks to make
the chapter’s organizing work more accessible to people of color and workers without
traditional, fixed working hours. We must struggle within DSA to sweep away all obstacles to full
working class participation and advance a liberatory, ecosocialist line.

# For Black and Brown power!
Amidst a pandemic that killed millions and an economic crisis that caused widespread
unemployment in the summer of 2020, the brave people of Minneapolis sparked an
international uprising against white supremacy and police brutality. Black people, indigenous
people, and people of color have a deep understanding that the neoliberal political order does
not serve them. However, TCDSA has thus far failed to connect with these communities, spelling
certain doom for many chapter initiatives. Regenerate believes that TCDSA must make every
possible effort to meet Black and Brown people where they are, organize communities of color,
and truly become a democratic organization of the multi-racial working class.
